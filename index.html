Featured Project

Tap to reveal image

This project was coded during my time working at Mobkoi. It is a template that allows designers to build captivating ad units for luxury clients and addresses limitations in the existing ad software, thus expanding the company’s product offerings. Tap to reveal shows an image when the user taps and holds hotspots on the screen. The image can either follow the finger movement or remain static. It also changes the call to action based on the user’s last clicked product, whilst operating seamlessly across various ad formats, mobile and desktop, over images or video.
