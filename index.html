Featured Project

Tap to reveal image

This project was coded during my time working at Mobkoi. It is a template that allows designers to build captivating ad units for luxury clients and it was developed to address limitations in the existing ad software, thus expanding the company’s product offerings. It also provides clients with dynamic ways to reveal their products images based on user interaction. Tap to reveal image reveals an image when the user taps and holds hotspots on the screen. The image can either follow the finger movement or remain static. It also changes the call to action based on the user’s last clicked product. It operates seamlessly across various ad formats, mobile and desktop, whether over images or video. The product achieved a 0.25% engagement rate.
